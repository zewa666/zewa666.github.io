Back from DWX

What a great event. I’ve been participating in this years Developer Week (DWX) conference in Nürnberg/Germany as a speaker. It was my first time giving a conf talk so I didn’t really know what to expect, but overall it was a extremely pleasant experience. Besides having the opportunity to speak about topics I’m interested in, I was able to meet a lot of people, sharing my focus on FrontEnd-Development.

There have been tons of interesting talks about Angular, .NET and several other topics. I gave two talks on Monday. First one was about Angular 1.x and EcmaScript 6, the second one, a last-minute backup presentation :), a short introduction of Aurelia. You can find more details here

The example demonstrated in the first talk can be found on GitHub.

As always, I’m looking forward to hearing some feedback. If you’ve been at the DWX as well, please let me know how you liked it in the comment section below.

Vildan Softic

Vildan Softic is a consultant and software developer from Austria. He is passionate about developing Single Page Applications, grinding LOB Apps with .NET and is pushing towards Node.JS development.
